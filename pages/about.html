Why transition into programming?

Just like many 17/18 year olds about to finish high school, I had no clue what I wanted to do with myself. 8 years later I found myself in a job I dreaded and decided I needed a change. I've always been interested in tech and knew it was where I wanted to be, it was only a matter of how I'd get there. After careful consideration of personal and financial factors I decided doing a bootcamp with Coder Academy was the best choice for me.

Previous work & study (RESUME)

After graduating with a Bachelor of Engineering(Civil) I worked as a wastewater engineer, designing wastewater management systems for developments without access to sewage mains.

I then worked as a stormwater engineer while learning the basics of coding to see if this was what I really wanted to do.
